FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an appointment?

Walk-ins are always welcome! However, we recommend calling ahead on weekends or if you have a specific time in mind to ensure minimal wait times.

How long does eyebrow threading take?

Eyebrow threading typically takes about 10-15 minutes, depending on the amount of shaping required.

Is threading painful?

Most clients experience only minor discomfort. Threading is generally less painful than waxing and becomes more comfortable with regular visits.

How long do results last?

Threading results typically last 2-4 weeks, depending on your individual hair growth cycle.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, credit cards, and debit cards at both locations.

Can I get threading if I have sensitive skin?

Yes! Threading is great for sensitive skin because it doesn't use chemicals or hot wax. Let your technician know about any skin sensitivities.
