The Art of Threading

Why Threading?

Threading is an ancient hair removal technique that originated in South Asia. Using only a twisted cotton thread, our artists can remove hair at the follicle level with unmatched precision. Unlike waxing, threading doesn't pull at the skin, making it ideal for sensitive areas and all skin types. The result? Cleaner lines, longer-lasting smoothness, and naturally beautiful brows.
