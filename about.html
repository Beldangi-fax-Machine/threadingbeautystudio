Our Story

Threading Beauty Studio

Threading Beauty Studio, operated by DOMA THAPA LTD, was founded with a simple mission: to bring the ancient art of threading to Vermont while providing exceptional customer service and beautiful results.

What started as a single location has grown to serve the communities of both Winooski and Burlington. Our passion for precision and dedication to enhancing natural beauty has made us a trusted destination for threading services in the area.

We believe that everyone deserves to feel confident and beautiful. Our skilled technicians combine traditional threading techniques with modern aesthetics to create the perfect look for each individual client.

The Art of Threading

Our Approach

Threading is an ancient hair removal technique that originated in Asia and the Middle East. At Threading Beauty Studio, we've mastered this art form to deliver precise, clean, and long-lasting results.
